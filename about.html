About PINOW - Preserving Nature Through Needle & Thread

Established in 2021 by Evelyn, PINOW Studio was born from a love for the great outdoors and a passion for preserving nature through the art of hand embroidery and macramé. Here at PINOW, we celebrate the magic of nature and the joy of creating with our own hands. Whether you're seeking a mental oasis, the satisfaction of a handcrafted piece, or the pleasure of a hobby that has been cherished for centuries, you'll find a welcoming space here at PINOW. We invite you to explore our studio and join us on this creative journey.
